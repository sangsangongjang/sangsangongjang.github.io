Privacy Policy for Pop Blur

Effective date: April 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Pop Blur (also referred to as the "App", "Application", "we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use our mobile application.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at sang.san.gong.jang@gmail.com.

1. Overview

Pop Blur is a photo editing application that allows users to apply blur, mosaic, brush, pen, stickers, and related editing effects to images on their devices.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how information is handled when you use the App.

2. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the App, we and our third-party service providers may process the following types of information:

2.A. Photos and Media You Select

When you choose a photo in the App, the App accesses that image so it can be edited and saved.

  • Selected photos are used to provide the core editing features of the App.
  • Photo editing is performed locally on your device.
  • We do not intentionally upload your selected photos to our own servers for editing or storage.
